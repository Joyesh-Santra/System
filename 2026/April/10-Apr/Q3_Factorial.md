## Factorial

Given a non-negative integer `n`, compute `n!` (n factorial).

**Input:** A single integer `n`.

**Output:** Print `n!`.

**Constraints:** `0 ≤ n ≤ 20`


### 💡 Examples

**Example 1:**
- **Input:** `5`
- **Output:** `120`
- **Explanation:** 5! = 5×4×3×2×1 = 120

**Example 2:**
- **Input:** `0`
- **Output:** `1`
- **Explanation:** 0! = 1 by definition
